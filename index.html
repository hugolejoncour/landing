My coaching experiences

As part of a project led by the BIAT Foundation (International Arab Bank of Tunisia) for youth, I volunteered in humanitarian action during 2017 for a period of three months every Saturday. This consisted of supervising in learning, children from disadvantaged backgrounds in remote regions of Tunisia. As for me, I gave a few French lessons as well as some basic math lessons to a group of young students. This action immediately struck me when I heard about it, it allowed the underprivileged youth of the country to have access to a minimum of education. This experience allowed me to realize that some young people were motivated by the idea of ​​learning but that their financial means did not allow it. The fight against inequality in education prompted me to participate in this humanitarian action because for me every child deserves to have access to education regardless of their social background. I think that only educated and cultivated youth can allow a country like Tunisia to develop.

My civic engagement

I was part of the Unesco Club of Lycée Pierre Mendès France with which I participated in many actions of all kinds. These actions had only been carried out within the establishment, mainly through awareness raising campaigns. Of all the actions, one really stood out; it was our campaign to fight violence against women. From posters to dancing, speeches to song, it was very touching for me to see the investment our group has shown. It was the experience that made me realize that I was helping to change mentalities. Finally this club made me mature and showed me that I too could leave my mark on this earth and this is the most important. In my future, I would like to have a constant reminder of humility such as Unesco, in order to never forget that union is the force !
